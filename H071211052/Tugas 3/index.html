WE ARE VALORANT


DEFY THE LIMITS

Blend your style and experience on a global, competitive stage. You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side using sharp gunplay and tactical abilities. And, with one life per-round, you'll need to think faster than your opponent if you want to survive. Take on foes across Competitive and Unranked modes as well as Deathmatch and Spike Rush.

Connoisseurs of personal computer (PC) games with the first person shooter (FPS) genre were again surprised by the appearance of Valorant. Valorant is an FPS game developed and released by a game developer from the United States named Riot Games and officially released on June 2, 2020. When Valorant was released in Indonesia, Riot Games collaborated with one of the leading esports event organizers in Indonesia, One Up.
