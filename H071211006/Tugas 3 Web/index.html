About Us

Take an indie-rock band and inject it with a firm attitude and catchy phrases, that's Reality Club!

Established in late 2016, Reality Club is an AMI (Anugerah Musik Indonesia) nominated indie band mogul who were nominated for best alternative track of the year and best newcomer of 2018.

Initially, the band was formed by Fathia Izzati, Era Patigo, and Mayo in 2016. But recently Fathia invited her sister, Faiz Novascotia Saripudin, and her ex-lover, Iqbal Anggakusumah to join. But unfortunately, Mayo decided to leave after six months of being formed due to personal activities and was replaced by Nugi Wicaksono.

Formed in Jakarta, they have been ever-growing and catching people's eyes and ears since their first release.

Their album has many songs that might help raise your mood, accompany you in the middle of traffic jams, clean the house and others.
