What Is Hogwarts?

Hogwarts Dormitory is a dormitory used by students at the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry as a place to live while studying at Hogwarts, the division of this dormitory is based on a selection made by the selection hat. In the Harry Potter series, the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry is divided into four houses, each bearing the surname of its founders Godric Gryffindor, Salazar Slytherin, Rowena Ravenclaw and Helga Hufflepuff. The houses compete with each other during their time in school, adding or subtracting numbers from various events, to earn the Boarding Cup. Each has its own Quidditch team competing for the Quidditch Cup. The most frequent (and most exciting) competition and rivalry between Gryffindor House and Slytherin, which even in the story tends to lead to hostility. The hostel at Hogwarts is a place to live and study for every student.



Gryffindor is one of the fictional house names in J. K. Rowling's Harry Potter series. Gryffindor House is at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Gryffindor House's identity animal is a lion. Red and gold are Gryffindor's proud colors. The name was taken from one of the founders of the school, Godric Gryffindor. He was one of the greatest wizards of his time. Gryffindor lived about a thousand years ago with Rowena Ravenclaw, Helga Hufflepuff, and Salazar Slytherin. Gryffindor's present remains are the selection hat that once belonged to him and a sword studded with jewels and engraved with his name which is now kept in Professor Albus Dumbledore's office.


Hufflepuff, founded by Helga Hufflepuff, values hard work, patience, loyalty, dedication, tolerance and fairness above all. The boarding identity animals are the badger and the canary with yellow and black as their proud colors. The Fat Friar is a dorm keeper ghost. According to Rowling, Hufflepuff corresponds to the earth element, where the dormitory where they live is located in the basement, near the Hogwarts kitchen.

Ravenclaw highly values intelligence, creativity, resourcefulness, wisdom, and individuality. "Infinite intelligence is man's most precious treasure" is a quote frequently repeated by Ravenclaw. The boarding symbol is an eagle, and uses blue and bronze colors (although in the film blue and silver are used). The house ghost is the Gray Lady, who is revealed in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows as Helena Ravenclaw, the daughter of Hogwarts founder Rowena Ravenclaw. According to Rowling, Ravenclaw is related to the element of air. Rowena Ravenclaw is also the owner of the crown, which is later proven to be a horcrux in this seventh book. If you pay close attention throughout the story series, the majority of the students who live in this dormitory can be said to be beautiful and attractive, like Cho Chang, Harry's first girlfriend. Then, Luna Lovegood is also said to be beautiful in the sixth book, at Slughorn's Christmas party.



Slytherin like it's founder, Salazar Slytherin, the Slytherin house judges on ambition, cunning (cunning), leadership talent, long wits, tendencies to break rules and (on average) pure blood ancestry. The book also states that the thirst for power is a characteristic of Slytherins. The animal that symbolizes Slytherin is a snake, and uses green and silver as identity colors. The Bloody Baron is the ghost of the Slytherin house. The dorm's common room is also located underground, but it's different from Hufflepuff's. Slytherin's basement is located beneath the lake giving the room a greenish tinge, this room is also equipped with green lights and carved chairs. According to Rowling, Slytherin is associated with the element of water. Slytherin traits are prone to being misused for crime.
